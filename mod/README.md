# More Legacies

**10 new dynasty legacy tracks for Crusader Kings III**

## What This Mod Adds

### 10 New Legacy Tracks

Each legacy track contains 5 perks that must be unlocked in order.

1. **Chivalry** - Courtly warrior dynasty (knights, romance, prestige)
2. **Devotion** - Religious dynasty (piety, churches, holy orders)
3. **Dominance** - Tyrannical dynasty (dread, military, fear)
4. **Industry** - Development dynasty (building, innovation, growth)
5. **Influence** - Political dynasty (schemes, vassals, intrigue)
6. **Mercantile** - Merchant dynasty (trade, wealth, diplomacy)
7. **Seafaring** - Maritime dynasty (naval, exploration, coastal)
8. **Temptation** - Hedonistic dynasty (fertility, seduction, genetics)
9. **Tradition** - Conservative dynasty (homogeneity, stability, culture)
10. **Witchcraft** - Occult dynasty (witches, covens, dark arts)

### 50 New Perks

Each legacy has 5 perks (10 legacies × 5 perks = 50 total).

## How to Access

### In-Game

1. Open **Dynasty** window
2. Select **Legacies** tab
3. Choose available legacy track
4. Unlock perks with Renown (dynasty head only, must unlock in order 1→2→3→4→5)

### Legacy Availability

**Always Visible (4 legacies):**
- Devotion
- Industry
- Influence
- Temptation

**Culture-Dependent (6 legacies):**

**Chivalry** - Requires one of:
- Ethos: Bellicose or Courtly
- Traditions: Hereditary Hierarchy, Only the Strong, Chivalry, Chanson de Geste

**Dominance** - Requires one of:
- Ethos: Bellicose or Stoic
- Traditions: Ruling Caste, Castle Keepers, Staunch Traditionalists, Talent Acquisition, Swords for Hire, Only the Strong, Eye for an Eye, Martial Admiration, Warrior Culture, Performative Honour, The Right to Prove, Trials by Combat, By the Sword

**Mercantile** - Requires one of:
- Ethos: Bureaucratic or Egalitarian
- Traditions: Astute Diplomats, Family Entrepreneurship, Maritime Mercantilism, Artisans, Xenophilic

**Seafaring** - Requires one of:
- Traditions: Maritime Mercantilism, Coastal Warriors, Fishermen, Practiced Pirates, Seafaring, Polders

**Tradition** - Requires:
- Does NOT have Ethos: Egalitarian
- Does NOT have Traditions: Culture Blending, Malleable Subjects, Malleable Invaders, Xenophilic, Language Scholars, Religion Blending, African Tolerance, Steppe Tolerance

**Witchcraft** - Requires:
- Character has Witch trait OR secret_witch secret

## Legacy Descriptions

### Chivalry Legacy

1. **Romantic**: +10 attraction opinion, faster courting schemes
2. **Valiant**: +10% prestige gain, +15% knight effectiveness
3. **Poetic**: +10 courtier/guest opinion, +5 court grandeur, +50% grandeur growth, faster language learning
4. **Gallant**: +1 knight limit, +0.2 prestige per knight/month, +0.2 piety per knight/month
5. **Heroic**: +1 diplomacy per prestige level, +1 prowess per prestige level

### Devotion Legacy

1. **Righteous**: +5% piety per happy powerful vassal, +5 tolerance opinion
2. **Venerate**: +15% domain tax from same faith
3. **Sanctify**: -10% church building speed/cost
4. **Unwavering**: -20% holy order hire cost, +1% piety per knight
5. **Ardent**: +1 learning per piety level, +10% same-faith levy reinforcement

### Dominance Legacy

1. **Ruthless**: +20% dread gain, +10% pursuit efficiency, +10% enemy casualties
2. **Oppressive**: +10 baseline dread, +10% war income
3. **Dark Rumors**: -20% dread decay, +0.05 prestige per dread/month
4. **Intimidating**: -10% men-at-arms maintenance, +10% intimidated vassal levies, +20% cowed vassal levies
5. **Brutal**: +1 martial per prestige level, +1 prowess per stress level

### Industry Legacy

1. **Earnest Efforts**: -25% short reign duration, +30 days enemy hostile scheme duration
2. **Ever-Improving**: -10% building speed, +20% development growth
3. **Always Learning**: +10 councillor opinion, +15% cultural fascination (if cultural head)
4. **Meticulous**: +1 domain limit, +5 county opinion
5. **Perseverance**: +1 learning per stress level, +1 stewardship per stress level, +5% income per stress level

### Influence Legacy

1. **Power Dynamics**: +10 vassal limit, +5 vassal opinion, +5 courtier opinion, +10 fellow vassal opinion, +10 liege opinion
2. **Astute**: +0.1 county control growth/month, +10% vassal tax
3. **Cunning**: Faster befriend/sway/murder schemes
4. **Popular Persona**: +5 county opinion, -0.05 tyranny decay/month
5. **Trustworthy**: +1 intrigue per prestige level

### Mercantile Legacy

1. **International Trade**: +15% diplomatic range, +10 independent ruler opinion, +5 court grandeur
2. **Artisans**: +15% domain tax, -5% building speed/cost
3. **Guarded Roads**: +1 knight limit, -20% men-at-arms maintenance, -20% mercenary hire cost
4. **Investors**: +20% happy powerful vassal tax
5. **Established Routes**: +15% diplomatic range, +25% long reign bonus, +1 stewardship per prestige level

### Seafaring Legacy

1. **Sea Legs**: +1 prowess, no water crossing penalty, -25% embarkation cost
2. **Well-Traveled**: +30% diplomatic range, +10 different culture opinion
3. **Sea Breezes**: -10% stress gain, +10% stress loss, +20% naval movement speed
4. **Maritime Law**: +10% war income, +5 coastal advantage
5. **Bountiful Seas**: -20% army maintenance, +20% supply duration/capacity

### Temptation Legacy

1. **Fruitful Labors**: +10% fertility, +5 years of fertility
2. **Comforts of Home**: +5 court grandeur, +10 spouse opinion, +10% stress loss
3. **Prolific Flirt**: Faster seduce/courting/elope schemes
4. **Refined Tastes**: +10% good trait inheritance, -10% bad trait inheritance, +10% trait strengthening
5. **Sweet Corruption**: +1 intrigue per stress level, +1 max seduce schemes

### Tradition Legacy

1. **Enduring**: +25% long reign bonus, -10% title creation cost, +10% same-faith domain tax
2. **Unified Front**: +5 baseline dread, +5 dynasty/house opinion, +10 close relative opinion
3. **Customary**: +15% prestige gain, +15% piety gain
4. **Like-Minded**: +5 same culture/faith opinion, -20% same-culture holy order/mercenary hire cost
5. **Established**: +1 diplomacy per piety level, +30 days enemy personal scheme duration

### Witchcraft Legacy

1. **Profane Rites**: +10% fertility, +5 years life expectancy
2. **Initiation**: Can convert 3 people to witchcraft simultaneously, much faster conversion
3. **Sorcerous Studies**: +15% cultural fascination (if cultural head), faster language learning
4. **Otherworldly Aura**: +0.1 piety per dread/month, +5 baseline dread
5. **Dark Mysticism**: +1 intrigue per piety level
